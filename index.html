About

Luminia 2K26 is a vibrant and inspiring literature fest organized by the Class Union of Midad mates 24th Batch at Darunnajath Islamic Complex. The fest celebrates the beauty of language, creativity, and intellectual expression by bringing together students through various literary and cultural activities. Designed to nurture talent and encourage a love for literature, Luminia 2K26 serves as a platform for young minds to share ideas, showcase their skills, and create lasting memories. With enthusiasm, unity, and a passion for learning, the Midad mates 24th Batch proudly presents this event as a remarkable chapter in the literary journey of the college community.
